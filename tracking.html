Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my package?

You can track your package by entering your tracking number in the form above. Your tracking number is a 12-character alphanumeric code that was provided in your shipping confirmation email or receipt.

What if I lost my tracking number?

If you've lost your tracking number, you can find it in your shipping confirmation email or by logging into your FastShip account. If you're the recipient, please contact the sender to request the tracking number.

Why hasn't my tracking information updated?

Tracking information is typically updated when your package arrives at a new facility or when a delivery attempt is made. There may be periods where your package is in transit between facilities and no updates are available. If your tracking hasn't updated for more than 48 hours, please contact customer support.

Can I change my delivery address?

Yes, you can request a delivery address change for most packages that haven't yet been delivered. Use the "Change Delivery Address" option in the Delivery Options section after tracking your package. Additional fees may apply for this service.

What does "Out for Delivery" mean?

"Out for Delivery" means your package has been loaded onto a delivery vehicle and is scheduled to be delivered that day. Delivery times vary based on your location and the driver's route.

What is a delivery exception?

A delivery exception occurs when an unexpected event delays delivery of your package. This could include weather conditions, incomplete address information, or no one available to sign for the package. The tracking information will provide specific details about the exception and next steps.

Have more questions? Visit our complete FAQ section or contact our support team.
